About Me

Prelude

TL;DR: I got into university as a Physics graduate, and ended up a Programmer. Oops!

It has always been an obsession for me to learn the "How"s & "Why"s of our world, and Science serves as a great tool for answering a whole bunch of those. Computer Science has been no different!

My first introduction to code was on my first year at university. And Boy has that been influencial! Being able to think in code opened up a whole "Array" of perspectives about the world. When allowing to give your ideas a life in a world of 0s and 1s, you are only limited by your imagination!

The decision of starting to work out has been another great impact in my life. Not only could I stay fit and eat well (thus stay healthy), but I also became more confident in myself.
Being able to pick up weights that I never thought I could, so easily - serves as a very good reminder that hard work and consistency, along with good aim shows you in flesh that you are stronger than you thought!

There is no end to improvement, and, that has both pros and cons at the same time.
I'm very interested to learn more about Data Science and Machine Learning, as well as Entrepreneurship, Project Management, Software Engineering Principles, Music Theory, Philosophy, Cognitive Psychology, Underwater Basket Weaving and so much more that this para could go on forever.

Oh, I also started making crappy music covers on youtube recently.

BTW, I hate mosquitoes with a passion.
